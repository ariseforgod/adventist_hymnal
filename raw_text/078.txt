078 – For God So Loved Us


Verse 1
For God so loved us,
He sent the Savior:
For God so loved us,
and loves me too.

Chorus
Love so unending!
I’ll sing His praises,
God loves His children,
loves even me.

Verse 2
He sent the Savior,
the blest Redeemer;
He sent the Savior
to set me free.

Verse 3
He bade me welcome,
O word of mercy;
He bade me welcome,
O voice divine.

Verse 4
Glory and honor,
O Love eternal,
To Him be given
while life shall last.
